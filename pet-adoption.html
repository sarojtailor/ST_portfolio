Design Goal

Many pets are currently in shelters and pet-homes waiting to be adopted. Design a product/flow that will help people looking for a new pet to connect with a right pet for them. Help a pet lover find a pet which matches their lifestyle.

Read the entire process here

Solution

An app that provides:

- A list of pets from different shelters in your city according to your lifestyle match.
- The user will be able to chat and schedule a visit to the shelter where they can adopt the pet.
- A list of care guides available that are catered to the needs of your pet

Lifestyle Match

After the onboarding screens, to help the pet lover find a perfect match, the user is presented with a quiz about their lifestyle, personality, and living conditions.  This is done in best interest of both the pet and their prospective owners.

Pet Details

First time user will see a dedicated feed based on their preferences.  The detail page lists out the required traits along with other important information about the pet.  The user can contact the shelter by calling or chatting in the app with them. The details also list out the direction to reach the shelter.

Scheduling a visit

The user can schedule a visit to the shelter to spend some time with the animals before adopting them.

Care Guides

Care guides help new or current pet owners with all their queries. This might reduce some stress from the pet owners and help reduce the number of pets returned to the shelter.
